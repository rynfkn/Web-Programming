Foodies in Samarinda

Samarinda is a city that is rich in culture and tradition. The city is also known for its delicious food. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Samarinda:

Kerupuk Amplang

This crispy fish cracker is a popular snack in Samarinda. It is made from fish paste mixed with tapioca starch and shaped into small crunchy bites.

Pisang Gapit

Pisang Gapit is grilled banana served with a sweet sauce made from palm sugar and coconut milk. It's a delightful snack loved by locals.

Nasi Kuning

Nasi Kuning is a fragrant yellow rice dish made with turmeric, usually served with chicken, eggs, and a variety of side dishes.

Sate Payau

Sate Payau is deer meat satay, a unique delicacy of Samarinda that offers tender meat with a rich, flavorful marinade.
