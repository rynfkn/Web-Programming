City of Samarinda

Samarinda is the capital city of East Kalimantan province in Indonesia. It's located on the island of Borneo and is famous for its natural resources and rich culture.

The name Samarinda comes from the local Bugis language, where "samarenda" means "equal in height". This name refers to the traditional river houses built on rafts, where no house or person was higher than the other.
